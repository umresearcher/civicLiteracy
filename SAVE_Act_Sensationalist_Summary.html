What the SAVE Act Demands

The bill amends the National Voter Registration Act of 1993, closing what conservatives call a dangerous loophole that allows non-citizens to simply “check a box and sign their name” to vote—no proof required. The SAVE Act requires REAL ID-compliant documentation like a passport or birth certificate and mandates states to purge non-citizens from voter rolls.
