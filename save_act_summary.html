Summary of SAVE Act Articles

The Safeguard American Voter Eligibility (SAVE) Act, introduced by Rep. Chip Roy (R-TX) and Sen. Mike Lee (R-UT), has been a topic of significant discussion and debate. The bill aims to amend the National Voter Registration Act of 1993 to require proof of U.S. citizenship to register to vote in federal elections.
